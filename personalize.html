Our Fabrics
We pick our denim from the best mills to offer you a wide range of options to fit your style and needs. Different colors and shades from rigid to stretch fabrics will allow you to find the perfect mix between style and comfort. We divide our fabrics in 4 different categories. Under each category you will find different fabrics, and with each fabric we can generate different shades.
All cotton used in our fabrics is BCI certified. All our providers go through strict controls to make sure they don’t use polluting chemicals or manufacturing processes. Every single jeans we manufacture is made using low water, low energy and low chemicals consumption, making them one of the most sustainable jeans to buy. No greenwashing. Facts.
